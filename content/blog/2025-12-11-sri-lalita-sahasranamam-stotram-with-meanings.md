**श्रीमाता श्रीमहाराज्ञी श्रीमत्-सिंहासनेश्वरी ।**\
**चिदग्नि-कुण्ड-सम्भूता देवकार्य-समुद्यता ॥१॥**

१. श्रीमाता 

She who is the auspicious Mother who gives immeasurable wealth, who removes all sorrows and gives only happiness — indicates also her role of creation.

२. श्रीमहाराज्ञी 

She who is the Empress of the Universe, she who takes care of the universe — indicates her role of protection.

३. श्रीमित्संहासनेश्वरी 

She who is the queen of the most glorious throne. She who sits on the most glorious throne of lions — indicates her role of destruction.

४. चिदग्निकुण्डसम्भूता 

She who rose from the fire-pit of pure consciousness and is the ultimate truth.

५. देवकायसमुद्यता 

She who is intent on helping and fulfilling the wishes of the gods.

**उद्यद्भानु-सहस्राभा चतुर्बाहु-समन्विता ।**\
**रागस्वरूप-पाशाढ्या क्रोधाकाराङ्कुशोज्ज्वला ॥२॥**

६. उद्यद्भानुसहस्राभा 

She who has the radiance of a thousand rising suns. 

७. चतुर्बाहुसमन्विता 

She who is four-armed. 

८. रागस्वरूपपाशाढ्या 

She who is holding the rope of love in her hand.

९. क्रोधाकाराङ्कुशोज्ज्वला 

She who glitters and has anger in the form of Anghusa (goad) –in one of her right hands.

**मनोरूपेक्षु-कोदण्डा पञ्चतन्मात्र-सायका ।**\
**निजारुण-प्रभापूर-मज्जद्ब्रह्माण्ड-मण्डला ॥३॥**

१०. मनोरूपेक्षुकोदण्डा 

She who holds in her hand a sugarcane bow that represents the mind.

११. पञ्चतन्मात्रसायका 

She who holds the five subtle elements of touch, smell, hearing, taste and sight as arrows. 

१२. निजारुणप्रभापूरमज्जद्ब्रह्माण्डमण्डला 

She who makes all the universe immerse in her red colour of her form, which is like the sun in the dawn.

**चम्पकाशोक-पुन्नाग-सौगन्धिक-लसत्कचा ।**\
**कुरुविन्दमणि-श्रेणी-कनत्कोटीर-मण्डिता ॥४॥**

१३. चम्पकाशोकपुन्नागसौगिन्धकलसत्कचा 

She whose hair has been adorned with flowers like champaka, ashoka, punnaga and saugandhika.

१४. कुरुविन्दमणिश्रेणीकनत्कोटीरमण्डिता 

She who is resplendent with a crown adorned with rows of precious kuruvinda gemstones. 

**अष्टमीचन्द्र-विभ्राज-दलिकस्थल-शोभिता ।**\
**मुखचन्द्र-कलङ्काभ-मृगनाभि-विशेषका ॥५॥**

१५. अष्टमीचन्द्रविभ्राजदलिकस्थलशोभिता 

She who has a beautiful forehead that shines like the crescent moon of the eighth night of the lunar half-month.

१६. मुखचन्द्रकलङ्काभमृगनाभिविशेषका

She who has the tilaka (dot mark) of Musk in her forehead which shines like the black shadow on the moon.

**वदनस्मर-माङ्गल्य-गृहतोरण-चिल्लिका ।**\
**वक्त्रलक्ष्मी-परीवाह-चलन्मीनाभ-लोचना ॥६॥**

१७. वदनस्मरमाङ्गल्यगृहतोरणचिल्लका 

She whose beautiful eyebrows look like ornaments on her face and which shine like the arches leading to the house of kama, the god of love.

१८. वक्त्रलक्ष्मीपरीवाहचलन्मीनाभलोचना 

She whose beautiful eyes possess the luster of the fish that move about in the stream of beauty flowing from the pond of her face. 

**नवचम्पक-पुष्पाभ-नासादण्ड-विराजिता ।**\
**ताराकान्ति-तिरस्कारि-नासाभरण-भासुरा ॥७॥**

१९. नवचम्पकपुष्पाभनासादण्डविराजिता

She who is resplendent with a nose that has the beauty of a newly blooming champaka flower.

२०. ताराकान्तितिरस्कारिनासाभरणभासुरा

She who shines with a nose-ring that excels the luster of a star.

**कदम्बमञ्जरी-कॢप्त-कर्णपूर-मनोहरा ।**\
**ताटङ्क-युगली-भूत-तपनोडुप-मण्डला ॥८॥**

२१. कदम्बमञ्जरीकॢप्तकर्णपूरमनोहरा

She who is captivating, wearing bunches of kadamba flowers as ear-ornaments. 

२२. ताटङ्कयुगलीभूततपनोडुपमण्डला 

She who wears the sun and the moon as a pair of large earrings.

**पद्मराग-शिलादर्श-परिभावि-कपोलभूः ।**\
**नवविद्रुम-बिम्बश्री-न्यक्कारि-रदनच्छदा ॥९॥**

२३. पद्मरागशिलादर्शपरिभाविकपोलभूः

She whose cheeks outshine mirrors made of rubies in their beauty. 

२४. नवविद्रुमबिम्बश्रीन्यक्कारिरदनच्छदा

She whose lips excel freshly cut coral and bimba fruit in their reflective splendor and beauty.

**शुद्ध-विद्याङ्कुराकार-द्विजपङ्क्ति-द्वयोज्ज्वला ।**\
**कर्पूर-वीटिकामोद-समाकर्षि-दिगन्तरा ॥१०॥**

२५. शुद्धविद्याङ्कुराकारद्विजपङ्क्तिद्वयोज्ज्वला

She who has radiant teeth which resemble the buds of pure knowledge.

२६. कर्पूरवीटिकामोदसमाकर्षिदिगन्तरा

She who is enjoying a camphor-laden betel roll, the fragrance of which is attracting people from all directions.

**निज-सल्लाप-माधुर्य-विनिर्भर्त्सित-कच्छपी ।**\
**मन्दस्मित-प्रभापूर-मज्जत्कामेश-मानसा ॥११॥**

२७. निजसल्लापमाधुर्यविनिर्भर्त्सितकच्छपी

She who excels even the veena of Sarasvati in the sweetness of her speech.

२८. मन्दस्मितप्रभापूरमज्जत्कामेशमानसा

She who submerges even the mind of Kamesha (Lord shiva) with the radiance of her smile.

**अनाकलित-सादृश्य-चिबुकश्री-विराजिता ।**\
**कामेश-बद्ध-माङ्गल्य-सूत्र-शोभित-कन्धरा ॥१२॥**

२९. अनाकलितसादृश्यचिबुकश्रीविराजिता

She whose chin cannot be compared to anything because of its unparalleled beauty.

३०. कामेशबद्धमाङ्गल्यसूत्रशोभितकन्धरा

She whose neck is adorned with the marriage thread tied by Kamesha.

**कनकाङ्गद-केयूर-कमनीय-भुजान्विता ।**\
**रत्नग्रैवेय-चिन्ताक-लोल-मुक्ता-फलान्विता ॥१३॥**

३१. कनकाङ्गदकेयूरकमनीयभुजान्विता

She whose arms are beautifully adorned with golden armlets.

३२. रत्नग्रैवेयचिन्ताकलोलमुक्ताफलान्विता

She whose neck is resplendent with a gem-studded necklace with a locket made of pearl.

**कामेश्वर-प्रेमरत्न-मणि-प्रतिपण-स्तनी ।**\
**नाभ्यालवाल-रोमालि-लता-फल-कुचद्वयी ॥१४॥**

३३. कामेश्वरप्रेमरत्नमणिप्रतिपणस्तनी

She who gives her breasts to Kameshvara in return for the gem of love he bestows on her.

३४. नाभ्यालवालरोमालिलताफलकुचद्वयी

She whose breasts are the fruits on the creeper of the fine hairline that starts in the depths of her navel and spreads upwards.

**लक्ष्यरोम-लताधारता-समुन्नेय-मध्यमा ।**\
**स्तनभार-दलन्मध्य-पट्टबन्ध-वलित्रया ॥१५॥**

३५. लक्ष्यरोमलताधारतासमुन्नेयमध्यमा 

She who has a waist, the existence of which can only be inferred by the fact that the creeper of her hairline springs from it.

३६. स्तनभारदलन्मध्यपट्टबन्धवलित्रया 

She whose abdomen has three folds which form a belt to support her waist from breaking under the weight of her breasts.

**अरुणारुण-कौसुम्भ-वस्त्र-भास्वत्-कटीतटी ।**\
**रत्न-किङ्किणिका-रम्य-रशना-दाम-भूषिता ॥१६॥**

३७. अरुणारुणकौसुम्भवस्त्रभास्वत्कटीतटी 

She whose hips are adorned with a garment as red as the rising sun, which is dyed with an extract from safflower (kusumbha) blossoms.

३८. रत्नकिङ्किणिकारम्यरशनादामभूषिता

She who is adorned with a girdle which is decorated with many gem-studded bells.

**कामेश-ज्ञात-सौभाग्य-मार्दवोरु-द्वयान्विता ।**\
**माणिक्य-मुकुटाकार-जानुद्वय-विराजिता ॥१७॥**

३९. कामेशज्ञातसौभाग्यमार्दवोरुद्वयान्विता

The beauty and softness of whose thighs are known only to Kamesha, her husband.

४०. माणिक्यमुकुटाकारजानुद्वयविराजिता

She whose knees are like crowns shaped from the precious red jewel, manikya (a kind of ruby).

**इन्द्रगोप-परिक्षिप्त-स्मरतूणाभ-जङ्घिका ।**\
**गूढगुल्फा कूर्मपृष्ठ-जयिष्णु-प्रपदान्विता ॥१८॥**

४१. इन्द्रगोपपरिक्षिप्तस्मरतूणाभजङ्घिका

She whose calves gleam like the jewel-covered quiver of the God of Love.

४२. गूढगुल्फा 

She whose ankles are hidden.

४३. कूर्मपृष्ठजयिष्णुप्रपदान्विता

She whose feet have arches that rival the back of a tortoise in smoothness and beauty.

**नख-दीधिति-संछन्न-नमज्जन-तमोगुणा ।**\
**पदद्वय-प्रभाजाल-पराकृत-सरोरुहा ॥१९॥**

४४. नखदीधितिसंछन्ननमज्जनतमोगुणा

She whose toenails give out such a radiance that all the darkness of ignorance is dispelled completely from those devotees who prostrate at her feet.

४५. पदद्वयप्रभाजालपराकृतसरोरुहा 

She whose feet defeat lotus flowers in radiance.

**सिञ्जान-मणिमञ्जीर-मण्डित-श्री-पदाम्बुजा ।**\
**मराली-मन्दगमना महालावण्य-शेवधिः ॥२०॥**

४६. सिञ्जानमणिमञ्जीरमण्डितश्रीपदाम्बुजा

She whose auspicious lotus feet are adorned with gem-studded golden anklets that tinkle sweetly.

४७. मरालीमन्दगमना 

She whose gait is as slow and gentle as that of a swan.

४८. महालावण्यशेवधिः

She who is the treasure-house of beauty.

**सर्वारुणा ऽनवद्याङ्गी सर्वाभरण-भूषिता ।**\
**शिव-कामेश्वराङ्कस्था शिवा स्वाधीन-वल्लभा ॥२१॥**

४९. सर्वारुणा

She who is entirely red in complexion.

५०. अनवद्याङ्गी 

She whose body is worthy of worship.

५१. सर्वाभरणभूषिता

She who is resplendent with all types of ornaments.

५२. शिवकामेश्वराङ्कस्था

She who sits in the lap of Shiva, who is the conqueror of desire.

५३. शिवा

She who bestows all that is auspicious.

५४. स्वाधीनवल्लभा 

She who keeps her husband always under her control.

**सुमेरु-मध्य-श‍ृङ्गस्था श्रीमन्नगर-नायिका ।**\
**चिन्तामणि-गृहान्तस्था पञ्च-ब्रह्मासन-स्थिता ॥२२॥**

५५. सुमेरुमध्यशृङ्गस्था 

She who sits on the middle peak of Mount Sumeru.

५६. श्रीमन्नगरनायिका

She who is the Mistress of the most auspicious (or prosperous).

५७. चिन्तामणिगृहान्तस्था

She who resides in a house built of the chintamani.

५८. पञ्चब्रह्मासनस्थिता

She who sits on a seat made of five Brahmas.

**महापद्माटवी-संस्था कदम्बवन-वासिनी ।**\
**सुधासागर-मध्यस्था कामाक्षी कामदायिनी ॥२३॥**

५९. महापद्माटवीसंस्था 

She who resides in the great lotus forest.

६०. कदम्बवनवासिनी

She who resides in the kadamba forest.

६१. सुधासागरमध्यस्था 

She who resides in the center of the ocean of nectar.

६२. कामाक्षी 

She whose eyes awaken desire, or she who has beautiful eyes.

६३. कामदायिनी

She who grants all wishes.

**देवर्षि-गण-संघात-स्तूयमानात्म-वैभवा ।**\
**भण्डासुर-वधोद्युक्त-शक्तिसेना-समन्विता ॥२४॥**

६४. देवर्षिगणसंघातस्तूयमानात्मवैभवा

She whose might is the subject of praise by multitudes of gods and sages.

६५. भण्डासुरवधोद्युक्तशिक्तसेनासमिन्वता 

She who is endowed with an army of shaktis intent on slaying bhandasura.

**सम्पत्करी-समारूढ-सिन्धुर-व्रज-सेविता ।**\
**अश्वारूढाधिष्ठिताश्व-कोटि-कोटिभिरावृता ॥२५॥**

६६. सम्पत्करीसमारूढसिन्धुरव्रजसेविता

She who is attended by a herd of elephants ably commanded by Sampatkari.

६७. अश्वारूढाधिष्ठिताश्वकोटिकोटिभिरावृता

She who is surrounded by a cavalry of several million horses which are under the command of the shakti, Ashvarudha.

**चक्रराज-रथारूढ-सर्वायुध-परिष्कृता ।**\
**गेयचक्र-रथारूढ-मन्त्रिणी-परिसेविता ॥२६॥**

६८. चक्रराजरथारूढसर्वायुधपरिष्कृता

She who shines in her chariot Chakraraja, equipped with all kinds of weapons.

६९. गेयचक्ररथारूढमन्त्रिणीपरिसेविता

She who is served by the shakti named Mantrini, who rides the chariot known as Geyacakra.

**किरिचक्र-रथारूढ-दण्डनाथा-पुरस्कृता ।**\
**ज्वाला-मालिनिकाक्षिप्त-वह्निप्राकार-मध्यगा ॥२७॥**

७०. किरिचक्ररथारूढदण्डनाथापुरस्कृता

She who is escorted by the shakti named Dandanatha, seated in the chariot known as Kirichakra.

७१. ज्वालामालिनिकाक्षिप्तवह्निप्राकारमध्यगा

She who has taken position at the center of the fortress of fire created by the goddess,Jvalamalini.

**भण्डसैन्य-वधोद्युक्त-शक्ति-विक्रम-हर्षिता ।**\
**नित्या-पराक्रमाटोप-निरीक्षण-समुत्सुका ॥२८॥**

७२. भण्डसैन्यवधोद्युक्तशक्तिविक्रमहर्षिता

She who rejoices at the valor of the shaktis who are intent on destroying the forces of Bhandasura.

७३. नित्यापराक्रमाटोपनिरीक्षणसमुत्सुका

She who delights in seeing the might and the pride of her Nitya deities.

**भण्डपुत्र-वधोद्युक्त-बाला-विक्रम-नन्दिता ।**\
**मन्त्रिण्यम्बा-विरचित-विषङ्ग-वध-तोषिता ॥२९॥**

७४. भण्डपुत्रवधोद्युक्तबालाविक्रमनन्दिता

She who delights in seeing the valor of the Bala Devi (her daughter) who is intent on killing the sons of Bhandasur.

७५. मन्त्रिण्यम्बाविरचितविषङ्गवधतोषिता

She who rejoices at the destruction, in battle, of the demon Vishanga (a brother of Bhandasura, who is personification of physical desires) by the Devi Mantrini. 

**विशुक्र-प्राणहरण-वाराही-वीर्य-नन्दिता ।**\
**कामेश्वर-मुखालोक-कल्पित-श्रीगणेश्वरा ॥३०॥**

७६. विशुक्रप्राणहरणवाराहीवीर्यनन्दिता

She who is pleased with the valour of Varahiv who took the life of Vishukra (another brother of Bhandasura, who is personification of ignorance).

७७. कामेश्वरमुखालोककल्पितश्रीगणेश्वरा

She who created Ganesha by her mere glance at the face of her Lord , Kameshwara.

**महागणेश-निर्भिन्न-विघ्नयन्त्र-प्रहर्षिता ।**\
**भण्डासुरेन्द्र-निर्मुक्त-शस्त्र-प्रत्यस्त्र-वर्षिणी ॥३१॥**

७८. महागणेशनिर्भिन्नविघ्नयन्त्रप्रहर्षिता

She who rejoices at seeing Lord Ganesha shatter all obstacles (the Vigna Yantra, created by Vishukra meant to delay things).

७९. भण्डासुरेन्द्रनिर्मुक्तशस्त्रप्रत्यस्त्रवर्षिणी

She who rained arrows as counter weapons against Bhandasura.

**कराङ्गुलि-नखोत्पन्न-नारायण-दशाकृतिः ।**\
**महा-पाशुपतास्त्राग्नि-निर्दग्धासुर-सैनिका ॥३२॥**

८०. कराङ्गुलिनखोत्पन्ननारायणदशाकृतिः

She who created all the ten incarnations of Narayana (Vishnu) from her fingernails . \[when Bandasura sent the Sarvasura Astra (arrow), she destroyed it by creating the ten avatars of Vishnu from the tip of her fingernails].

८१. महापाशुपतास्त्राग्निनिर्दग्धासुरसैनिका

She who burned the armies of the asuras in the fire of the missile, by the Maha Pasupatha arrow.

**कामेश्वरास्त्र-निर्दग्ध-सभण्डासुर-शून्यका ।**\
**ब्रह्मोपेन्द्र-महेन्द्रादि-देव-संस्तुत-वैभवा ॥३३॥**

८२. कामेश्वरास्त्रनिर्दग्धसभण्डासुरशून्यका

She who burned and destroyed Bhandasura and his capital Shunyaka with the Kameshvara missile.

८३. ब्रह्मोपेन्द्रमहेन्द्रादिदेवसंस्तुतवैभवा

She whose many powers are extolled by Brahma, Vishnu, Shiva and other gods.

**हर-नेत्राग्नि-संदग्ध-काम-सञ्जीवनौषधिः ।**\
**श्रीमद्वाग्भव-कूटैक-स्वरूप-मुख-पङ्कजा ॥३४॥**

८४. हरनेत्राग्निसंदग्धकामसञ्जीवनौषधिः

She who became the life-giving medicine for Kamadeva (the god of love) who had been burned to ashes by the fire from Shiva’s third eye.

८५. श्रीमद्वाग्भवकूटैकस्वरूपमुखपङ्कजा 

She whose lotus face is the auspicious Vagbhava  Koota (a group of syllables of the Panchadashi mantra).

**कण्ठाधः-कटि-पर्यन्त-मध्यकूट-स्वरूपिणी ।**\
**शक्ति-कूटैकतापन्न-कट्यधोभाग-धारिणी ॥३५॥**

८६. कण्ठाधःकटिपर्यन्तमध्यकूटस्वरूपिणी

She who from her neck to her waist is of the form of the Madhya Koota (the middle six syllables of the Panchadasakshari mantra).

८७. शक्तिकूटैकतापन्नकट्यधोभागधारिणी

She whose form below the waist is the Shakti Koota (the last four syllables of the Panchadasakshari mantra).

**मूल-मन्त्रात्मिका मूलकूटत्रय-कलेबरा ।**\
**कुलामृतैक-रसिका कुलसंकेत-पालिनी ॥३६॥**

८८. मूलमन्त्रात्मिका

She who is the embodiment of the Moola mantra (the Panchadasakshari mantra).

८९. मूलकूटत्रयकलेबरा

She whose (subtle) body is made of the three parts of the Panchadasakshari mantra.

९०. कुलामृतैकरसिका

She who is especially fond of the nectar known as kula.

९१. कुलसंकेतपालिनी

She who protects the code of rituals of the path of yoga known as kula.

**कुलाङ्गना कुलान्तस्था कौलिनी कुलयोगिनी ।**\
**अकुला समयान्तस्था समयाचार-तत्परा ॥३७॥**

९२. कुलाङ्गना 

She who is well-born (who is from a good family).

९३. कुलान्तस्था 

She who resides in the kula vidya. 

९४. कौलिनी

She who belongs to the kula.

९५. कुलयोगिनी

She who is the deity in the kulas.

९६. अकुला 

She who does not have a family.

९७. समयान्तस्था 

She who resides inside ‘samaya’.

९८. समयाचारतत्परा

She who likes Samayachara i.e. worship  stepwise from the Mooladhara Chakra.

**मूलाधारैक-निलया ब्रह्मग्रन्थि-विभेदिनी ।**\
**मणि-पूरान्तरुदिता विष्णुग्रन्थि-विभेदिनी ॥३८॥**

९९. मूलाधारैकनिलया

She whose principal abode is the Mooladhara.

१००. ब्रह्मग्रन्थिविभेदिनी

She who breaks through the knot of Brahma.

१०१. मणिपूरान्तरुदिता

She who emerges in the Manipura Chakra. 

१०२. विष्णुग्रन्थिविभेदिनी

She who breaks through the knot of Vishnu.

**आज्ञा-चक्रान्तरालस्था रुद्रग्रन्थि-विभेदिनी ।**\
**सहस्राराम्बुजारूढा सुधा-साराभिवर्षिणी ॥३९॥**

१०३. आज्ञाचक्रान्तरालस्था 

She who resides at the center of the Aj~nA Chakra.

१०४. रुद्रग्रन्थिविभेदिनी

She who breaks through the knot of Shiva.

१०५. सहस्राराम्बुजारूढा 

She who ascends to the thousand-petaled lotus.

१०६. सुधासाराभिवर्षिणी

She who pours out streams of ambrosia.

**तडिल्लता-समरुचिः षट्चक्रोपरि-संस्थिता ।**\
**महासक्तिः कुण्डलिनी बिसतन्तु-तनीयसी ॥४०॥**

१०७. तडिल्लतासमरुचिः

She who is as beautiful as a flash of lightning.

१०८. षट्चक्रोपरिसंस्थिता

She who resides above the six chakras.

१०९. महासक्तिः 

She who is greatly attached to the festive union of Shiva and Shakti, and who likes worship by her devotees.

११०. कुण्डलिनी

She who is in the form Kundalini (a form which is a snake hissing and exists in mooladhara).

१११. बिसतन्तुतनीयसी

She who is fine and delicate as the fiber of the lotus.

**भवानी भावनागम्या भवारण्य-कुठारिका ।**\
**भद्रप्रिया भद्रमूर्तिर् भक्त-सौभाग्यदायिनी ॥४१॥**

११२. भवानी 

She who is the wife of bhava (Shiva).

११३. भावनागम्या 

She who is approachable and attainable through imagination or thought.

११४. भवारण्यकुठारिका

She who is like an axe to clear the jungle of samsara.

११५. भद्रप्रिया

She who is fond of all auspicious things – who gives all auspicious things.

११६. भद्रमूर्तिर्

She who is the embodiment of auspiciousness or benevolence.

११७. भक्तसौभाग्यदायिनी

She who confers prosperity on her devotees.

**भक्तिप्रिया भक्तिगम्या भक्तिवश्या भयापहा ।**\
**शाम्भवी शारदाराध्या शर्वाणी शर्मदायिनी ॥४२॥**

११८. भक्तिप्रिया

She who is fond of (and pleased by) devotion.

११९. भक्तिगम्या

She who is attained only through devotion.

१२०. भक्तिवश्या

She who is to be won over by devotion.

१२१. भयापहा 

She who dispels fear.

१२२. शाम्भवी 

She who is the wife of Shambhu (Shiva).

१२३. शारदाराध्या 

She who is worshipped by Sharada (Sarasvati, the goddess of speech).

१२४. शर्वाणी

She who is the wife of Sharva (Shiva).

१२५. शर्मदायिनी

She who confers pleasures.

**शाङ्करी श्रीकरी साध्वी शरच्चन्द्र-निभानना ।**\
**शातोदरी शान्तिमती निराधारा निरञ्जना ॥४३॥**

१२६. शाङ्करी 

She who is the wife of Shankara.

१२७. श्रीकरी 

She who bestows riches in abundance, all forms of wealth and happiness.

१२८. साध्वी 

She who is eternally devoted to her husband.

१२९. शरच्चन्द्रनिभानना

She whose face shines like the full moon in the clear sky of the Sharad season (autumn). 

१३०. शातोदरी 

She who is slender-waister or she has a thin belly.

१३१. शान्तिमती

She who is peace personified.

१३२. निराधारा

She who is without any dependence.

१३३. निरञ्जना

She who is devoid of any blemishes or scars.

**निर्लेपा निर्मला नित्या निराकारा निराकुला ।**\
**निर्गुणा निष्कला शान्ता निष्कामा निरुपप्लवा ॥४४॥**

१३४. निर्लेपा

She who stays unattached, bound to nothing.

१३५. निर्मला

She who is free from all impurities.

१३६. नित्या

She who is eternally stable.

१३७. निराकारा

She from whom all forms arise or she who is without a form.

१३८. निराकुला

She who is always calm and has utmost clarity.

१३९. निर्गुणा

She who is beyond all three gunas of nature, namely sattva,rajas and tamas.

१४०. निष्कला

She who is without any division.

१४१. शान्ता 

She who is tranquil.

१४२. निष्कामा

She who is without any desire.

१४३. निरुपप्लवा

She who is indestructible.

**नित्यमुक्ता निर्विकारा निष्प्रपञ्चा निराश्रया ।**\
**नित्यशुद्धा नित्यबुद्धा निरवद्या निरन्तरा ॥४५॥**

१४४. नित्यमुक्ता

She who is forever free from worldly ties and bonds.

१४५. निर्विकारा

She who is unchanging.

१४६. निष्प्रपञ्चा

She who is beyond this universe.

१४७. निराश्रया

She who does not need any support on anything.

१४८. नित्यशुद्धा

She who is eternally pure.

१४९. नित्यबुद्धा

She who is ever wise.

१५०. निरवद्या

She who can never be accused of anything.

१५१. निरन्तरा

She who is all-pervading, continuous.

**निष्कारणा निष्कलङ्का निरुपाधिर् निरीश्वरा ।**\
**नीरागा रागमथनी निर्मदा मदनाशिनी ॥४६॥**

१५२. निष्कारणा

She who does not have a cause.

१५३. निष्कलङ्का

She who is faultless.

१५४. निरुपाधिर्

She who is not conditioned or has no limitations.

१५५. निरीश्वरा

She who has no superior or protector, or she who does not have anyone controlling her.

१५६. नीरागा 

She who has no desire.

१५७. रागमथनी 

She who removes desires from us. 

१५८. निर्मदा

She who is without any pride and beliefs.

१५९. मदनाशिनी

She who destroys all pride and beliefs.

**निश्चिन्ता निरहंकारा निर्मोहा मोहनाशिनी ।**\
**निर्ममा ममताहन्त्री निष्पापा पापनाशिनी ॥४७॥**

१६०. निश्चिन्ता

She who has no anxiety in anything.

१६१. निरहंकारा

She who is without any ego. She who is without the concept of ‘I’ and ‘mine’.

१६२. निर्मोहा

She who is free from delusion.

१६३. मोहनाशिनी

She who destroys delusion in her devotees.

१६४. निर्ममा

She who has no self-interest in anything.

१६५. ममताहन्त्री 

She who destroys the sense of ownership.

१६६. निष्पापा

She who is without sin.

१६७. पापनाशिनी

She who destroys all the sins of her devotees. 

**निष्क्रोधा क्रोधशमनी निर्लोभा लोभनाशिनी ।**\
**निःसंशया संशयघ्नी निर्भवा भवनाशिनी ॥४८॥**

१६८. निष्क्रोधा

She who is without anger.

१६९. क्रोधशमनी 

She who destroys all the anger in her devotees.

१७०. निर्लोभा

She who is without greed.

१७१. लोभनाशिनी

She who destroys greed in her devotees.

१७२. निःसंशया

She who is without doubts.

१७३. संशयघ्नी 

She who kills all doubts.

१७४. निर्भवा

She who is without origin.

१७५. भवनाशिनी

She who destroys the sorrow of samsara (the cycle of birth and death).

**निर्विकल्पा निराबाधा निर्भेदा भेदनाशिनी ।**\
**निर्नाशा मृत्युमथनी निष्क्रिया निष्परिग्रहा ॥४९॥**

१७६. निर्विकल्पा

She who does not do anything she does not desire.

१७७. निराबाधा

She who is not affected or disturbed by anything.

१७८. निर्भेदा

She who is beyond all sense of difference.

१७९. भेदनाशिनी

She who removes from her devotees off of all sense of differences born of vasanas and promotes oneness.

१८०. निर्नाशा

She who never dies.

१८१. मृत्युमथनी 

She who destroys death and removes the fear of death.

१८२. निष्क्रिया

She who remains without action.

१८३. निष्परिग्रहा

She who does not acquire or accept any help or anything at all.

**निस्तुला नीलचिकुरा निरपाया निरत्यया ।**\
**दुर्लभा दुर्गमा दुर्गा दुःखहन्त्री सुखप्रदा ॥५०॥**

१८४. निस्तुला

She who is incomparable, unequalled.

१८५. नीलचिकुरा

She who has shining black hair.

१८६. निरपाया

She who is imperishable.

१८७. निरत्यया

She who does not cross the limits of the rules she herself created.

१८८. दुर्लभा

She who is difficult to obtain.

१८९. दुर्गमा

She who can not be neared easily, and is approachable only with extreme effort.

१९०. दुर्गा

She who is the Goddess Durga.

१९१. दुःखहन्त्री 

She who is the destroyer of sorrow.

१९२. सुखप्रदा 

She who is the giver of pleasure and happiness.

**दुष्टदूरा दुराचार-शमनी दोषवर्जिता ।**\
**सर्वज्ञा सान्द्रकरुणा समानाधिक-वर्जिता ॥५१॥**

१९३. दुष्टदूरा 

She who is unapproachable by sinners.

१९४. दुराचारशमनी 

She who stops evil customs.

१९५. दोषवर्जिता

She who is free from all faults.

१९६. सर्वज्ञा

She who is omniscient, knows everything.

१९७. सान्द्रकरुणा 

She who shows intense compassion and mercy.

१९८. समानाधिकवर्जिता

She who has neither equal nor superior; incomparable.

**सर्वशक्तिमयी सर्व-मङ्गला सद्गतिप्रदा ।**\
**सर्वेश्वरी सर्वमयी सर्वमन्त्र-स्वरूपिणी ॥५२॥**

१९९. सर्वशक्तिमयी

She who has all the divine powers (she who is omnipotent).

२००. सर्वमङ्गला

She who is the source of all that is auspicious.

२०१. सद्गतिप्रदा

She who leads into the right path.

२०२. सर्वेश्वरी

She who rules over all, living and non-living.

२०३. सर्वमयी

She who is everywhere, pervades every living and non-living thing.

२०४. सर्वमन्त्रस्वरूपिणी 

She who is the personification of all the mantras.

**सर्व-यन्त्रात्मिका सर्व-तन्त्ररूपा मनोन्मनी ।**\
**माहेश्वरी महादेवी महालक्ष्मीर् मृडप्रिया ॥५३॥**

२०५. सर्वयन्त्रात्मिका

She who is the soul of all yantras.

२०६. सर्वतन्त्ररूपा

She who is the soul (embodiment) of all tantras.

२०७. मनोन्मनी 

She who is Shiva’s Shakti.

२०८. माहेश्वरी 

She who is the wife of Maheshvara.

२०९. महादेवी 

She who is the wife of Mahadeva.

२१०. महालक्ष्मीर् 

She who takes the form of Goddess Lakshmi.

२११. मृडप्रिया

She who is the beloved of Mrida (Shiva).

**महारूपा महापूज्या महापातक-नाशिनी ।**\
**महामाया महासत्त्वा महाशक्तिर् महारतिः ॥५४॥**

२१२. महारूपा 

She who has a great mountainous form.

२१३. महापूज्या 

She who is the greatest object of worship.

२१४. महापातकनाशिनी

She who destroys even the greatest of sins.

२१५. महामाया 

She who is the great illusion.

२१६. महासत्त्वा 

She who possesses great sattva, a great knower of everything.

२१७. महाशक्तिर्

She who has great power.

२१८. महारतिः

She who is a boundless delight.

**महाभोगा महैश्वर्या महावीर्या महाबला ।**\
**महाबुद्धिर् महासिद्धिर् महायोगेश्वरेश्वरी ॥५५॥**

२१९. महाभोगा 

She who enjoys great pleasures.

२२०. महैश्वर्या

She who has immense wealth.

२२१. महावीर्या

She who is supreme in valor.

२२२. महाबला 

She who is supreme in might.

२२३. महाबुद्धिर्

She who is supreme in intelligence.

२२४. महासिद्धिर्

She who is endowed with the highest attainments.

२२५. महायोगेश्वरेश्वरी 

She who is the goddess of the greatest of yogis.

**महातन्त्रा महामन्त्रा महायन्त्रा महासना ।**\
**महायाग-क्रमाराध्या महाभैरव-पूजिता ॥५६॥**

२२६. महातन्त्रा 

She who is worshipped by the great Tantras. 

२२७. महामन्त्रा 

She who is the greatest mantra.

२२८. महायन्त्रा

She who is in the form of the great yantras.

२२९. महासना 

She who is seated on the greatest seat.

२३०. महायागक्रमाराध्या 

She who is worshipped by performing the ritual of Maha Yajna.

२३१. महाभैरवपूजिता

She who is worshipped even by Maha Bhairava (Shiva).

**महेश्वर-महाकल्प-महाताण्डव-साक्षिणी ।**\
**महाकामेश-महिषी महात्रिपुर-सुन्दरी ॥५७॥**

२३२. महेश्वरमहाकल्पमहाताण्डवसाक्षिणी 

She who is the witness of the great dance of Maheshvara (Shiva) at the end of the great cycle of creation.

२३३. महाकामेशमहिषी

She who is the great queen of Maha Kameshvara (Shiva).

२३४. महात्रिपुरसुन्दरी

She who is the great Tripurasundari, the queen of the three cities or the three lokas.

**चतुषष्ट्युपचाराढ्या चतुषष्टिकलामयी ।**\
**महाचतुषष्टिकोटि-योगिनी-गणसेविता ॥५८॥**

२३५. चतुषष्ट्युपचाराढ्या 

She who is adored and worshipers with sixty-four ceremonies or offerings.

२३६. चतुषष्टिकलामयी

She who embodies the sixty-four fine arts.

२३७. महाचतुषष्टिकोटियोगिनीगणसेविता

She who is attended (served) by sixty-four categories of bands of yoginis.

**मनुविद्या चन्द्रविद्या चन्द्रमण्डल-मध्यगा ।**\
**चारुरूपा चारुहासा चारुचन्द्र-कलाधरा ॥५९॥**

२३८. मनुविद्या

She who is the personification Sri Vidya as expounded by Manu.

२३९. चन्द्रविद्या

She who is the personification Sri Vidya as expounded by Moon.

२४०. चन्द्रमण्डलमध्यगा 

She who resides at the center of Chandramandala, the moon’s disc.

२४१. चारुरूपा 

She who has a beautiful face and form.

२४२. चारुहासा 

She who has a beautiful smile.

२४३. चारुचन्द्रकलाधरा 

She who wears of beautiful crescent moon that does not wax or wane.

**चराचर-जगन्नाथा चक्रराज-निकेतना ।**\
**पार्वती पद्मनयना पद्मराग-समप्रभा ॥६०॥**

२४४. चराचरजगन्नाथा 

She who is the ruler of all the moving and immobile things.

२४५. चक्रराजनिकेतना

She who lives in the centre of the Sri Chakra.

२४६. पार्वती

She who is the daughter of the Mountain (Mount Himavat or Himalaya).

२४७. पद्मनयना 

She who has eyes that are long and beautiful like the petals of the lotus flower.

२४८. पद्मरागसमप्रभा 

She who has a resplendent red complexion like the Padma Raga jewel (ruby).

**पञ्च-प्रेतासनासीना पञ्चब्रह्म-स्वरूपिणी ।**\
**चिन्मयी परमानन्दा विज्ञान-घनरूपिणी ॥६१॥**

२४९. पञ्चप्रेतासनासीना 

She who sits on the seat formed by the five corpses (of Brahma, Vishnu, Rudra, Eesa and Sadasiva; without their consort).

२५०. पञ्चब्रह्मस्वरूपिणी

She whose form is composed of the five brahmas (Brahma, Vishnu, Rudra, Eesa and Sadashiva; with their consort).

२५१. चिन्मयी

She who is the supreme consciousness.

२५२. परमानन्दा 

She who is the supreme bliss.

२५३. विज्ञानघनरूपिणी

She who is the embodiment of all-pervading science-based intelligence.

**ध्यान-ध्यातृ-ध्येयरूपा धर्माधर्म-विवर्जिता ।**\
**विश्वरूपा जागरिणी स्वपन्ती तैजसात्मिका ॥६२॥**

२५४. ध्यानध्यातृध्येयरूपा 

She who is the personification of meditation, the being that meditates and what is being meditated upon.

२५५. धर्माधर्मविवर्जिता

She who is devoid of both virtue and vice, beyond Dharma and Adharma.

२५६. विश्वरूपा

She who has the whole universe as her form.

२५७. जागरिणी

She who is in the waking state, or she who assumes the form of the jiva who is in the waking state.

२५८. स्वपन्ती 

She who is in the dream state, or she who assumes the form of the jiva who is in the dream state.

२५९. तैजसात्मिका

She who is the soul of taijasva/subconscious state, or she who assumes the form of the jiva who is in the subconscious state.

**सुप्ता प्राज्ञात्मिका तुर्या सर्वावस्था-विवर्जिता ।**\
**सृष्टिकर्त्री ब्रह्मरूपा गोप्त्री गोविन्दरूपिणी ॥६३॥**

२६०. सुप्ता 

She who is in the deep-sleep state or assumes the form of the jiva experiencing deep sleep.

२६१. प्राज्ञात्मिका

She who is awake.

२६२. तुर्या

She who is in the state of turya/trance (fourth state in which the ultimate realization of Atman is obtained).

२६३. सर्वावस्थाविवर्जिता

She who transcends all states.

२६४. सृष्टिकर्त्री

She who is the creator.

२६५. ब्रह्मरूपा 

She who is in the form of the supreme reality

२६६. गोप्त्री 

She who protects.

२६७. गोविन्दरूपिणी

She who has assumed the form of govinda (Vishnu) for the preservation of the universe.

**संहारिणी रुद्ररूपा तिरोधानकरी ईश्वरी ।**\
**सदाशिवा ऽनुग्रहदा पञ्चकृत्य-परायणा ॥६४॥**

२६८. संहारिणी

She who is the destroyer of the universe.

२६९. रुद्ररूपा 

She who has assumed the form of Rudra (Shiva). २७०. तिरोधान

She who causes the disappearance of all things.

२७१. ईश्वरी 

She who protects and rules everything, the ultimate Eeshwara.

२७२. सदाशिवा

She who is Sadashiva, one who always bestows auspiciousness.

२७३. अनुग्रहदा 

She who confers blessing.

२७४. पञ्चकृत्यपरायणा 

She who is devoted to the five functions (of creation, preservation, destruction, disappearance and reappearance).

**भानुमण्डल-मध्यस्था भैरवी भगमालिनी ।**\
**पद्मासना भगवती पद्मनाभ-सहोदरी ॥६५॥**

२७५. भानुमण्डलमध्यस्था 

She who abides at the center of the sun’s universe, the solar disc.

२७६. भैरवी 

She who is the wife of Bhairava (Shiva).

२७७. भगमालिनी

She who wears a garland made of the six excellences (of auspiciousness, supremacy, fame, valor, detachment and knowledge).

२७८. पद्मासना 

She who is seated in the lotus flower.

२७९. भगवती 

She who the goddess of wealth and knowledge

२८०. पद्मनाभसहोदरी 

She who is the sister of Vishnu.

**उन्मेष-निमिषोत्पन्न-विपन्न-भुवनावली ।**\
**सहस्र-शीर्षवदना सहस्राक्षी सहस्रपात् ॥६६॥**

२८१. उन्मेषनिमिषोत्पन्नविपन्नभुवनावली

She who creates and d stroys universes with the opening and closing of her eyelids.

२८२. सहस्रशीषवदना 

She who has a thousand heads and faces.

२८३. सहस्राक्षी 

She who has a thousand eyes.

२८४. सहस्रपात् 

She who has a thousand feet.

**आब्रह्म-कीट-जननी वर्णाश्रम-विधायिनी ।**\
**निजाज्ञारूप-निगमा पुण्यापुण्य-फलप्रदा ॥६७॥**

२८५. आब्रह्मकीटजननी 

She who is the creator and mother of everything , from a worm to God Brahma.

२८६. वर्णाश्रमविधायिनी

She who established the four fold order of the social division in life.

२८७. निजाज्ञारूपनिगमा

She whose commands take the form of the Vedas.

२८८. पुण्यापुण्यफलप्रदा 

She who dispenses the fruits of both good and evil actions.

**श्रुति-सीमन्त-सिन्दूरी-कृत-पादाब्ज-धूलिका ।**\
**सकलागम-सन्दोह-शुक्ति-सम्पुट-मौक्तिका ॥६८॥**

२८९. श्रुतिसीमन्तसिन्दूरीकृतपादाब्जधूलिका

She who is the one the dust from whose feet forms the vermillion marks at the parting line of the hair of the shruti devatas of the Vedas (goddesses of the Vedas).

२९०. सकलागमसन्दोहशुक्तिसम्पुटमौक्तिका

She who is the pearl enclosed in the shell made of all the Vedic scriptures.

**पुरुषार्थप्रदा पूर्णा भोगिनी भुवनेश्वरी ।**\
**अम्बिका अनादिनिधना हरिब्रह्मेन्द्र-सेविता ॥६९॥**

२९१. पुरुषार्थप्रदा

She who grants the (four-fold) objects of human life.

२९२. पूर्णा

She who is always complete by herself.

२९३. भोगिनी

She who is the enjoyer of great pleasures.

२९४. भुवनेश्वरी 

She who is the goddess presiding over the universe.

२९५. अम्बिका

She who is the mother of the universe.

२९६. अनादिनिधना

She who has neither beginning nor end.

२९७. हरिब्रह्मेन्द्रसेविता

She who is served by Brahma, Vishnu and Indra.

**नारायणी नादरूपा नामरूप-विवर्जिता ।**\
**ह्रींकारी ह्रीमती हृद्या हेयोपादेय-वर्जिता ॥७०॥**

२९८. नारायणी 

She who is the female equivalent of Narayana.

२९९. नादरूपा 

She who is in the form of sound.

३००. नामरूपविवर्जिता

She who has no name or form.

३०१. ह्रीङ्कारी 

She who is the form of the syllable ‘Hrim’.

३०२. ह्रीमती 

She who is endowed with modesty and shyness.

३०३. हृद्या 

She who abides in the heart of her devotees.

३०४. हेयोपादेयवर्जिता

She who has nothing to reject or accept.

**राजराजार्चिता राज्ञी रम्या राजीवलोचना ।**\
**रञ्जनी रमणी रस्या रणत्किङ्किणि-मेखला ॥७१॥**

३०५. राजराजार्चिता

She who is worshipped by the king of kings.

३०६. राज्ञी 

She who is the queen of Shiva, the God of all kings.

३०७. रम्या 

She who is the personification of unseen beauty.

३०८. राजीवलोचना 

She whose eyes are like rajiva (lotus flower).

३०९. रञ्जनी 

She who delights the mind.

३१०. रमणी 

She who is charming and awakens sweet emotions, she who plays with her devotees.

३११. रस्या 

She who feeds juice of everything.

३१२. रणत्किङ्किणिमेखला

She who wears a golden waistband with tinkling bells.

**रमा राकेन्दुवदना रतिरूपा रतिप्रिया ।**\
**रक्षाकरी राक्षसघ्नी रामा रमणलम्पटा ॥७२॥**

३१३. रमा 

She who has taken the form of Lakshmi.

३१४. राकेन्दुवदना 

She who has a delightful face like the full moon.

३१५. रतिरूपा

She who is in the form of rati, the wife of Kama.

३१६. रतिप्रिया

She who is fond of rati; she who is served by rati.

३१७. रक्षाकरी 

She who is the protector.

३१८. राक्षसघ्नी 

She who is the slayer of the entire race of demons.

३१९. रामा 

She who is the giver of delight.

३२०. रमणलम्पटा 

She who is devoted and keen to make love to the Lord of her heart, Lord shiva.

**काम्या कामकलारूपा कदम्ब-कुसुम-प्रिया ।**\
**कल्याणी जगतीकन्दा करुणा-रस-सागरा ॥७३॥**

३२१. काम्या 

She who is the personification of love.

३२२. कामकलारूपा 

She who is the personification of the art of love.

३२३. कदम्बकुसुमप्रिया

She who is especially fond of kadamba flowers.

३२४. कल्याणी 

She who bestows auspiciousness.

३२५. जगतीकन्दा 

She who is the root of all the universes.

३२६. करुणारससागरा 

She who is the ocean of compassion and mercy.

**कलावती कलालापा कान्ता कादम्बरीप्रिया ।**\
**वरदा वामनयना वारुणी-मद-विह्वला ॥७४॥**

३२७. कलावती 

She who is the embodiment of all arts.

३२८. कलालापा 

She whose speech is artful, sweet and musical.

३२९. कान्ता 

She who glitters and is beautiful.

३३०. कादम्बरीप्रिया

She who is fond of mead (honey wine).

३३१. वरदा 

She who grants boons generously.

३३२. वामनयना 

She who has beautiful eyes.

३३३. वारुणीमदविह्वला

She who is intoxicated by varuni, the wine of happiness (ambrosial drink).

**विश्वाधिका वेदवेद्या विन्ध्याचल-निवासिनी ।**\
**विधात्री वेदजननी विष्णुमाया विलासिनी ॥७५॥**

३३४. विश्वाधिका

She who transcends the universe.

३३५. वेदवेद्या 

She who can be understood through the Vedas.

३३६. विन्ध्याचलनिवासिनी

She who resides in the Vindhya mountains.

३३७. विधात्री

She who creates and sustains the universe.

३३८. वेदजननी 

She who is the mother of the Vedas.

३३९. विष्णुमाया

She who is the illusory power of Vishnu.

३४०. विलासिनी

She who is playful.

**क्षेत्रस्वरूपा क्षेत्रेशी क्षेत्र-क्षेत्रज्ञ-पालिनी ।**\
**क्षयवृद्धि-विनिर्मुक्ता क्षेत्रपाल-समर्चिता ॥७६॥**

३४१. क्षेत्रस्वरूपा 

She whose body is the personification of matter.

३४२. क्षेत्रेशी 

She who is the wife of Kshetresha (Shiva); the goddess of matter.

३४३. क्षेत्रक्षेत्रज्ञपालिनी

She who is the protector of matter and the knower of matter, therefore the protector of body and soul.

३४४. क्षयवृद्धिविनिर्मुक्ता

She who is free from growth and decay.

३४५. क्षेत्रपालसमर्चिता

She who is worshipped by Kshetrapala (Shiva in infant form).

**विजया विमला वन्द्या वन्दारु-जन-वत्सला ।**\
**वाग्वादिनी वामकेशी वह्निमण्डल-वासिनी ॥७७॥**

३४६. विजया

She who is ever-victorious.

३४७. विमला

She who is without a trace of ignorance, impurity and illusion.

३४८. वन्द्या 

She who is worshipped by everybody.

३४९. वन्दारुजनवत्सला 

She who is full of motherly love for those who worship her.

३५०. वाग्वादिनी

She who uses words with great effect in arguments.

३५१. वामकेशी 

She who has beautiful hair.

३५२. वह्निमण्डलवासिनी

She who resides in the universe of fire, which is Mooladhara.

**भक्तिमत्-कल्पलतिका पशुपाश-विमोचिनी ।**\
**संहृताशेष-पाषण्डा सदाचार-प्रवर्तिका ॥७८॥**

३५३. भक्तिमत्कल्पलतिका

She who is the kalpa (wish-granting) creeper to her devotees.

३५४. पशुपाशविमोचिनी

She who releases the ignorant from bondage.

३५५. संहृताशेषपाषण्डा

She who destroys all those who have left their faith.

३५६. सदाचारप्रवर्तिका

She who is immersed in (and inspires others to follow) and makes things happen through right conduct.

**तापत्रयाग्नि-सन्तप्त-समाह्लादन-चन्द्रिका ।**\
**तरुणी तापसाराध्या तनुमध्या तमोपहा ॥७९॥**

३५७. तापत्रयाग्निसन्तप्तसमाह्लादनचन्द्रिका

She who is like pleasure-giving moonlight that gives joy to those who suffer from the three types of pain.

३५८. तरुणी 

She who is ever young.

३५९. तापसाराध्या 

She who is worshipped by sages.

३६०. तनुमध्या 

She who is slender-waisted (with a narrow hip).

३६१. तमोपहा 

She who removes the ignorance born of tamas.

**चिति तत्पद-लक्ष्यार्था चिदेकरस-रूपिणी ।**\
**स्वात्मानन्द-लवीभूत-ब्रह्माद्यानन्द-सन्ततिः ॥८०॥**

३६२. चिति

She who is the personification of pure intelligence.

३६३. तत्पदलक्ष्यार्था

She who is the embodiment of truth (which is indicated by the word ‘tat’).

३६४. चिदेकरसरूपिणी

She who is the cause as well as of the nature of all wisdom. 

३६५. स्वात्मानन्दलवीभूतब्रह्माद्यानन्दसन्ततिः

She who makes the wisdom & bliss of Brahma and others, insignificant compared to that of her own.

**परा प्रत्यक्चितीरूपा पश्यन्ती परदेवता ।**\
**मध्यमा वैखरीरूपा भक्त-मानस-हंसिका ॥८१॥**

३६६. परा 

She who is the supreme; she who transcends all.

३६७. प्रत्यक्चितीरूपा 

She who is of the nature of unmanifested consciousness; she who makes us look for wisdom inside.

३६८. पश्यन्ती 

She who is the second level of sound after para in the Svadhisthana chakra; she who sees everything within herself.

३६९. परदेवता 

She who is the supreme deity; she who gives power to all gods.

३७०. मध्यमा 

She who stays in the middle of everything.

३७१. वैखरीरूपा 

She who is in the form of vaikhari (sound in the manifested words, in audible form).

३७२. भक्तमानसहंसिका

She who is like a swan in the lake of minds of her devotees.

**कामेश्वर-प्राणनाडी कृतज्ञा कामपूजिता ।**\
**श‍ृङ्गार-रस-सम्पूर्णा जया जालन्धर-स्थिता ॥८२॥**

३७३. कामेश्वरप्राणनाडी 

She who is the very life source of Kameshvara (Shiva).

३७४. कृतज्ञा 

She who knows all actions of everyone as they occur.

३७५. कामपूजिता

She who is worshipped by God of love Kama, in the Mooladhara chakra.

३७६. श‍ृङ्गाररससम्पूर्णा

She who is filled with the essence of love.

३७७. जया 

She who is the personification of victory. 

३७८. जालन्धरिस्थता 

She who resides in the jalandhara pitha (in the throat region); she who is the purest of the pure.

**ओड्याणपीठ-निलया बिन्दु-मण्डलवासिनी ।**\
**रहोयाग-क्रमाराध्या रहस्तर्पण-तर्पिता ॥ ८३॥**

३७९. ओड्याणपीठनिलया

She whose abode is the center known as odyana (in the Aj~nA chakra).

३८०. बिन्दुमण्डलवासिनी

She who resides in the dot in the centre of the Sri Chakra.

३८१. रहोयागक्रमाराध्या 

She who is worshipped in secret through sacrificial rites.

३८२. रहस्तर्पणतर्पिता

She who is to be gratified by the secret rites of worship.

**सद्यःप्रसादिनी विश्व-साक्षिणी साक्षिवर्जिता ।**\
**षडङ्गदेवता-युक्ता षाड्गुण्य-परिपूरिता ॥ ८४॥**

३८३. सद्यःप्रसादिनी 

She who bestows her grace immediately.

३८४. विश्वसाक्षिणी

She who is witness to the whole universe.

३८५. साक्षिवर्जिता

She who has no other witness for herself.

३८६. षडङ्गदेवतायुक्ता 

She who her angas (heart,head, hair, eyes, armor and weapons) as six deities who attend to her.

३८७. षाड्गुण्यपरिपूरिता

She who is fully endowed with the six good qualities (prosperity, duty, wisdom, valour, wisdom and renunciation).

**नित्यक्लिन्ना निरुपमा निर्वाण-सुखदायिनी ।**\
**नित्या-षोडशिका-रूपा श्रीकण्ठार्ध-शरीरिणी ॥८५॥**

३८८. नित्यक्लिन्ना

She who is ever compassionate and merciful.

३८९. निरुपमा

She who is incomparable.

३९०. निर्वाणसुखदायिनी

She who confers the bliss of liberation.

३९१. नित्याषोडशिकारूपा

She who is the embodiment of the sixteen daily deities (i.e.,kameshvari, bhagmalini, nityaklinna, bherunda, vahnivasini, mahavajreshvari, shivaduti, tvarita, kulasundari, nitya, nilapatakini, vijaya, sarvamangala, jvalamalini, chitra and tripursundari)

३९२. श्रीकण्ठार्धशरीरिणी

She who possesses half of the body of Shrikantha (Shiva).She who is in the form of Ardha Narishvara.

**प्रभावती प्रभारूपा प्रसिद्धा परमेश्वरी ।**\
**मूलप्रकृतिर् अव्यक्ता व्यक्ताव्यक्त-स्वरूपिणी ॥८६॥**

३९३. प्रभावती 

She who is shining brilliantly.

३९४. प्रभारूपा 

She who is the personification of the light provided by supernatural powers. 

३९५. प्रसिद्धा

She who is famous and celebrated.

३९६. परमेश्वरी 

She who is the supreme and sovereign goddess.

३९७. मूलप्रकृतिर्

She who is the root cause of the entire universe.

३९८. अव्यक्ता 

She who is unmanifested.

३९९. व्यक्ताव्यक्तस्वरूपिणी

She who is both in the manifested and unmanifested forms.

**व्यापिनी विविधाकारा विद्याविद्या-स्वरूपिणी ।**\
**महाकामेश-नयन-कुमुदाह्लाद-कौमुदी ॥८७॥**

४००. व्यापिनी

She who is all-pervading.

४०१. विविधाकारा

She who has a multitude of forms.

४०२. विद्याविद्यास्वरूपिणी

She who is the form of both knowledge and ignorance.

४०३. महाकामेशनयनकुमुदाह्लादकौमुदी 

She who is like the full moon that opens the lotus-like eyes of Maha Kamesh. 

**भक्त-हार्द-तमोभेद-भानुमद्भानु-सन्ततिः ।**\
**शिवदूती शिवाराध्या शिवमूर्तिः शिवङ्करी ॥८८॥**

४०४. भक्तहार्दतमोभेदभानुमद्भानुसन्ततिः

She who is the sunbeam which dispels the darkness from the heart of her devotees.

४०५. शिवदूती

She for whom Shiva is the representative/messenger; she who is Shiva’s messenger.

४०६. शिवाराध्या

She who is worshipped by Shiva.

४०७. शिवमूर्तिः

She whose form is Shiva himself.

४०८. शिवङ्करी

She who confers auspiciousness, prosperity, and blissfulness. 

**शिवप्रिया शिवपरा शिष्टेष्टा शिष्टपूजिता ।**\
**अप्रमेया स्वप्रकाशा मनोवाचामगोचरा ॥८९॥**

४०९. शिवप्रिया

She who is the beloved of Shiva.

४१०. शिवपरा

She who is solely devoted to Shiva; she who has no other interest except Shiva.

४११. शिष्टेष्टा

She who is loved by the righteous; she who loves righteous people.

४१२. शिष्टपूजिता

She who is always worshipped by the righteous.

४१३. अप्रमेया 

She who is beyond any sensory measures.

४१४. स्वप्रकाशा 

She who is self-luminous.

४१५. मनोवाचामगोचरा 

She who is beyond the range of mind and speech.

**चिच्छक्तिश् चेतनारूपा जडशक्तिर् जडात्मिका ।**\
**गायत्री व्याहृतिः सन्ध्या द्विजबृन्द-निषेविता ॥९०॥**

४१६. चिच्छक्तिश्

She who is the power & strength of wisdom.

४१७. चेतनारूपा 

She who is the personification of pure consciousness behind all actions.

४१८. जडशक्तिर्

She who is the strength of the immobile; she who is the maya that has transformed itself as the power of creation.

४१९. जडात्मिका

She who is in the form of the immobile world.

४२०. गायत्री 

She who is the Gayatri mantra.

४२१. व्याहृतिः

She who is in the nature/grammar behind all utterances; she who presides over the power of speech.

४२२. सन्ध्या 

She who is in the form of twilight; who is in the form of union of Atman and Brahman.

४२३. द्विजबृन्दनिषेविता

She who is worshipped by all beings.

**तत्त्वासना तत्त्वमयी पञ्च-कोशान्तर-स्थिता ।**\
**निःसीम-महिमा नित्य-यौवना मदशालिनी ॥९१॥**

४२४. तत्त्वासना 

She who has tattvas (principles) as her seat; she who abides in tattva.

४२५. तत् 

She who is ‘That’, the supreme truth, brahman.

४२६. त्वं 

She who is ‘You’.

४२७. अयी 

She who is the Divine Mother.

४२८. पञ्चकोशान्तरिस्थता 

She who resides within the five holy sheaths (koshas).

४२९. निःसीममहिमा

She whose glory is limitless.

४३०. नित्ययौवना

She who is ever youthful.

४३१. मदशालिनी

She who is shining by her own exuberance.

**मदघूर्णित-रक्ताक्षी मदपाटल-गण्डभूः ।**\
**चन्दन-द्रव-दिग्धाङ्गी चाम्पेय-कुसुम-प्रिया ॥९२॥**

४३२. मदघूर्णितरक्ताक्षी

She who has rotating red eyes due to her exuberance. 

४३३. मदपाटलगण्डभूः 

She whose cheeks are rosy due to her continuous free-flowing actions.

४३४. चन्दनद्रवदिग्धाङ्गी 

She whose whole body is smeared with sandalwood paste.

४३५. चाम्पेयकुसुमप्रिया

She who is especially fond of champaka flowers.

**कुशला कोमलाकारा कुरुकुल्ला कुलेश्वरी ।**\
**कुलकुण्डालया कौल-मार्ग-तत्पर-सेविता ॥९३॥**

४३६. कुशला 

She who is the embodiment of skillfulness.

४३७. कोमलाकारा 

She who is the embodiment of grace in her form.

४३८. कुरुकुल्ला 

She who is the shakti in the form of Kurukulla Devi.

४३९. कुलेश्वरी 

She who is the goddess of the clan. (the triad of knower, the known and the knowledge).

४४०. कुलकुण्डालया 

She who abides in the kulakunda (the bindu at the center of the Muladhara chakra).

४४१. कौलमार्गतत्परसेविता

She who is worshipped by those devoted to the Kaula tradition.

**कुमार-गणनाथाम्बा तुष्टिः-पुष्टिर्-मतिर्-धृतिः ।**\
**शान्तिः स्वस्तिमती कान्तिर् नन्दिनी विघ्ननाशिनी ॥९४॥**

४४२. कुमारगणनाथाम्बा 

She who is the mother of Kumara (Subrahmanya) and Gananatha (Ganesha).

४४३. तुष्टिः

She who is ever content, and is the personification of happiness.

४४४. पुष्टिर्

She who is the personification of nourishment.

४४५. मतिर्

She who is the personification of wisdom.

४४६. धृतिः

She who is the personification of courage.

४४७. शान्तिः

She who is the personification of  tranquility.

४४८. स्वस्तिमती

She who is the ultimate truth.

४४९. कान्तिर्

She who is the personification of light.

४५०. नन्दिनी

She who gives delight; she who is the the personification of Nandino, the daughter of Kama Dhenu.

४५१. विघ्ननाशिनी

She who destroys all obstacles.

**तेजोवती त्रिनयना लोलाक्षी-कामरूपिणी ।**\
**मालिनी हंसिनी माता मलयाचल-वासिनी ॥९५॥**

४५२. तेजोवती 

She who is resplendent.

४५३. त्रिनयना

She who has the sun, moon and fire as her three eyes.

४५४. लोलाक्षीकामरूपिणी 

She who has wandering passionate eyes.

४५५. मालिनी

She who is wearing garlands.

४५६. हंसिनी

She who is surrounded by swans (the yogins who have reached great spiritual heights).

४५७. माता 

She who is the mother of the universe, the Divine Mother.

४५८. मलयाचलवासिनी

She who resides in the Malaya mountain.

**सुमुखी नलिनी सुभ्रूः शोभना सुरनायिका ।**\
**कालकण्ठी कान्तिमती क्षोभिणी सूक्ष्मरूपिणी ॥९६॥**

४५९. सुमुखी 

She who has a beautiful face.

४६०. नलिनी

She whose body is soft and tender like lotus petals.

४६१. सुभ्रूः 

She who has beautiful eyelids.

४६२. शोभना 

She who is always radiant, she who brings good things.

४६३. सुरनायिका

She who is the leader of the gods.

४६४. कालकण्ठी 

She who is the wife of the one (Shiva) who killed the god of death.

४६५. कान्तिमती

She who is the embodiment of ethereal radiance.

४६६. क्षोभिणी

She who creates high emotions in the mind, she who gets agitated.

४६७. सूक्ष्मरूपिणी

She who has a form that is too subtle to be perceived by the sense organs.

**वज्रेश्वरी वामदेवी वयोऽवस्था-विवर्जिता ।**\
**सिद्धेश्वरी सिद्धविद्या सिद्धमाता यशस्विनी ॥९७॥**

४६८. वज्रेश्वरी 

She who is vajreshvari (the goddess of diamonds), the sixth daily deity.

४६९. वामदेवी 

She who is the wife of Vama deva (Shiva).

४७०. वयोऽवस्थाविवर्जिता

She who does not change due to age (or with time).

४७१. सिद्धेश्वरी

She who is the goddess worshipped by the siddhas (saints with supernatural powers).

४७२. सिद्धविद्या

She who is the personification of the panchadasa mantra (fifteen-syllabled mantra), which is called Siddhavidya.

४७३. सिद्धमाता

She who is the mother of Siddhas.

४७४. यशस्विनी

She who is of unequalled renown.

**विशुद्धिचक्र-निलया ऽऽरक्तवर्णा त्रिलोचना ।**\
**खट्वाङ्गादि-प्रहरणा वदनैक-समन्विता ॥९८॥**

४७५. विशुद्धिचक्रनिलया

She who resides in the vishuddhi chakra (sixteen petalled lotus).

४७६. ऽऽरक्तवर्णा

She who is of slightly red (rosy) complexion.

४७७. त्रिलोचना

She who has three eyes.

४७८. खट्वाङ्गादिप्रहरणा

She who is armed with a khadga (a kind of sword) and other weapons.

४७९. वदनैकसमन्विता

She who possesses only one face.

**पायसान्नप्रिया त्वक्स्था पशुलोक-भयङ्करी ।**\
**अमृतादि-महाशक्ति-संवृता डाकिनीश्वरी ॥९९॥**

४८०. पायसान्नप्रिया

She who is especially fond of sweet rice (payasam).

४८१. त्वक्स्था 

She who is the deity of the organ of touch (skin), she who lives in the sensibility of the skin.

४८२. पशुलोकभयङ्करी 

She who fills with fear the mortal beings bound by worldly existence, the humans who behave like animals.

४८३. अमृतादिमहाशक्तिसंवृता

She who is surrounded by Maha Shaktis— Amrita, Karshini, Indrani, Eeshani,  Uma, Urdwa, Kesi etc.

४८४. डाकिनीश्वरी

She who is the Dakini yogini, the goddess of the south (denoting death).

**अनाहताब्ज-निलया श्यामाभा वदनद्वया ।**\
**दंष्ट्रोज्ज्वला ऽक्ष-मालादि-धरा रुधिरसंस्थिता ॥१००॥**

४८५. अनाहताब्जनिलया

She who resides in the Anahata chakra (the twelve petalled lotus in the heart).

४८६. श्यामाभा 

She who is greenish-black in complexion.

४८७. वदनद्वया 

She who has two faces.

४८८. दंष्ट्रोज्ज्वला 

She who has long protruding teeth.

४८९. ऽक्षमालादिधरा

She who is wearing garlands of rudraksha beads and other things.

४९०. रुधिरसंस्थिता

She who presides over the blood in the bodies of living beings.

**कालरात्र्यादि-शक्त्यौघ-वृता स्निग्धौदनप्रिया ।**\
**महावीरेन्द्र-वरदा राकिण्यम्बा-स्वरूपिणी ॥१०१॥**

४९१. कालरात्र्यादिशक्त्यौघवृता

She who is surrounded by shaktis like Kalaratri, Kandita and others. 

४९२. स्निग्धौदनप्रिया

She who is fond of rice offerings containing ghee, oil and other substances containing fats.

४९३. महावीरेन्द्रवरदा 

She who bestows boons on great warriors.

४९४. राकिण्यम्बास्वरूपिणी

She who is in the form of the Rakini yogini.

**मणिपूराब्ज-निलया वदनत्रय-संयुता ।**\
**वज्रादिकायुधोपेता डामर्यादिभिरावृता ॥१०२॥**

४९५. मणिपूराब्जनिलया

She who resides in the ten-petaled lotus in the Manipura chakra.

४९६. वदनत्रयसंयुता 

She who has three faces.

४९७. वज्रादिकायुधोपेता

She who holds the vajra (lightning bolt) and other weapons.

४९८. डामर्यादिभिरावृता

She who is surrounded by Damari and other attending yoginis.

**रक्तवर्णा मांसनिष्ठा गुडान्न-प्रीत-मानसा ।**\
**समस्तभक्त-सुखदा लाकिन्यम्बा-स्वरूपिणी ॥१०३॥**

४९९. रक्तवर्णा

She who is of the colour of blood in complexion.

५००. मांसनिष्ठा

She who presides over the flesh in living beings.

५०१. गुडान्नप्रीतमानसा 

She who is fond of sweet rice made with jaggery.

५०२. समस्तभक्तसुखदा 

She who confers happiness on all her devotees.

५०३. लाकिन्यम्बास्वरूपिणी

She who is in the form of the Lakini yogini.

**स्वाधिष्ठानाम्बु-जगता चतुर्वक्त्र-मनोहरा ।**\
**शूलाद्यायुध-सम्पन्ना पीतवर्णा ऽतिगर्विता ॥१०४॥**

५०४. स्वाधिष्ठानाम्बुजगता

She who resides in the six-petaled lotus in the Svadhisthana chakra.

५०५. चतुर्वक्त्रमनोहरा

She who has four beautiful faces.

५०६. शूलाद्यायुधसम्पन्ना 

She who possesses the trident and other weapons (i.e. noose, skull and abhaya).

५०७. पीतवर्णा

She who is golden in colour.

५०८. ऽतिगर्विता

She who is very proud.

**मेदोनिष्ठा मधुप्रीता बन्धिन्यादि-समन्विता ।**\
**दध्यन्नासक्त-हृदया काकिनी-रूप-धारिणी ॥१०५॥**

५०९. मेदोनिष्ठा

She who resides in the fatty layer in living beings.

५१०. मधुप्रीता 

She who is fond of honey and other offerings made with honey.

५११. बन्धिन्यादिसमन्विता

She who is accompanied by shaktis called Bandhini.

५१२. दध्यन्नासक्तहृदया 

She who is particularly fond of rice offerings made with curd.

५१३. काकिनीरूपधारिणी

She who is in the form of Kakini yogini.

**मूलाधाराम्बुजारूढा पञ्च-वक्त्रा ऽस्थि-संस्थिता ।**\
**अङ्कुशादि-प्रहरणा वरदादि-निषेविता ॥१०६॥**

५१४. मूलाधाराम्बुजारूढा 

She who is resident in the lotus in the Mooladhara chakra (the basic support).

५१५. पञ्चवक्त्रा 

She who has five faces.

५१६. ऽस्थिसंस्थिता

She who resides in the bones of living beings.

५१७. अङ्कुशादिप्रहरणा

She who holds the goad and other weapons.

५१८. वरदादिनिषेविता

She who is attended by Varada and other shaktis.

**मुद्गौदनासक्त-चित्ता साकिन्यम्बा-स्वरूपिणी ।**\
**आज्ञा-चक्राब्ज-निलया शुक्लवर्णा षडानना ॥१०७॥**

५१९. मुद्गौदनासक्तचित्ता

She who is particularly fond of rice offerings mixed with mudga, a lentil (green gram dal).

५२०. साकिन्यम्बास्वरूपिणी

She who is in the form of Sakini yogini.

५२१. आज्ञाचक्राब्जनिलया

She who resides in the two-petaled lotus in the Ajna chakra. 

५२२. शुक्लवर्णा

She who is white in color.

५२३. षडानना 

She who has six faces.

**मज्जासंस्था हंसवती-मुख्य-शक्ति-समन्विता ।**\
**हरिद्रान्नैक-रसिका हाकिनी-रूप-धारिणी ॥१०८॥**

५२४. मज्जासंस्था 

She who is the presiding deity of the bone marrow.

५२५. हंसवतीमुख्यशिक्तसमिन्वता 

She who is accompanied by the shaktis HamsavatI and Kshamavati (in the two petals of the lotus).

५२६. हरिद्रान्नैकरसिका

She who is fond of rice offerings seasoned with turmeric.

५२७. हाकिनीरूपधारिणी

She who is in the form of Hakini yogini.

**सहस्रदल-पद्मस्था सर्व-वर्णोप-शोभिता ।**\
**सर्वायुधधरा शुक्ल-संस्थिता सर्वतोमुखी ॥१०९॥**

५२८. सहस्रदलपद्मस्था 

She who resides in the thousand-petaled lotus.

५२९. सर्ववर्णोपशोभिता

She who is radiant in many colors.

५३०. सर्वायुधधरा

She who holds all the known weapons.

५३१. शुक्लसंस्थिता

She who resides in the shukla (semen).

५३२. सर्वतोमुखी

She who has faces turned in all directions.

**सर्वौदन-प्रीतचित्ता याकिन्यम्बा-स्वरूपिणी ।**\
**स्वाहा स्वधा ऽमतिर् मेधा श्रुतिः स्मृतिर् अनुत्तमा ॥११०॥**

५३३. सर्वौदनप्रीतचित्ता

She who is pleased by all offerings of rice.

५३४. याकिन्यम्बास्वरूपिणी

She who is in the form of the Yakini yogini.

५३५. स्वाहा 

She who is the personification of ‘swaha’ (the chant at the end of mantras while offering oblations to the fire in yajna ceremonies).

५३६. स्वधा 

She who is the personification of ‘swadha’ invocation at the end of mantras.

५३७. ऽमतिर्

She who is in the form of ignorance.

५३८. मेधा 

She who is in the form of wisdom.

५३९. श्रुतिः

She who is in the personification of the Vedas.

५४०. स्मृतिर्

She who is the personification of Smritis (works based on the meaning of Vedas).

५४१. अनुत्तमा 

She who is above all; she who is not excelled by anyone.

**पुण्यकीर्तिः पुण्यलभ्या पुण्यश्रवण-कीर्तना ।**\
**पुलोमजार्चिता बन्ध-मोचनी बन्धुरालका ॥१११॥**

५४२. पुण्यकीर्तिः

She whose fame is sacred or righteous.

५४३. पुण्यलभ्या 

She who is attained only by righteous souls and righteous deeds.

५४४. पुण्यश्रवणकीर्तना

She who bestows merit on anyone who listens to and who sings praises of her.

५४५. पुलोमजार्चिता

She who is worshipped by Pulomaja (Indra’s wife).

५४६. बन्धमोचनी 

She who is free from bonds; she who releases us from bondage.

५४७. बन्धुरालका

She who has wavy locks of hair.

**विमर्शरूपिणी विद्या वियदादि-जगत्प्रसूः ।**\
**सर्वव्याधि-प्रशमनी सर्वमृत्यु-निवारिणी ॥११२॥**

५४८. विमर्शरूपिणी

She who is the personification of vimarsha (reflection or meaning).

५४९. विद्या

She who is in the form of knowledge.

५५०. वियदादिजगत्प्रसूः

She who is the Mother of the universe, which is the aggregate of all the elements starting with the ether.

५५१. सर्वव्याधिप्रशमनी

She who removes all diseases and sorrows.

५५२. सर्वमृत्युनिवारिणी

She who guards her devotees from all kinds of death.

**अग्रगण्या ऽचिन्त्यरूपा कलिकल्मष-नाशिनी ।**\
**कात्यायनी कालहन्त्री कमलाक्ष-निषेविता ॥११३॥**

५५३. अग्रगण्या 

She who is to be considered the foremost.

५५४. ऽचिन्त्यरूपा

She who is of a form beyond the reach of thought.

५५५. कलिकल्मषनाशिनी

She who is the destroyer of the sins of the Kaliyuga (the dark ages).

५५६. कात्यायनी 

She who is the daughter of a sage named Katyayana; she who is takes the form of Katyayini in the Odyana peetha.

५५७. कालहन्त्री 

She who is the destroyer of time (death).

५५८. कमलाक्षनिषेविता

She in whom Vishnu takes refuge, she is worshipped by the lotus eyed Vishnu.

**ताम्बूल-पूरित-मुखी दाडिमी-कुसुम-प्रभा ।**\
**मृगाक्षी मोहिनी मुख्या मृडानी मित्ररूपिणी ॥११४॥**

५५९. ताम्बूलपूरतमुखी 

She whose mouth is full from chewing betel.

५६०. दाडिमीकुसुमप्रभा

She whose complexion is like a pomegranate bud.

५६१. मृगाक्षी 

She whose eyes are long and beautiful like those of a doe (a female deer).

५६२. मोहिनी

She who is enchanting.

५६३. मुख्या 

She who is the chief.

५६४. मृडानी 

She who gives pleasure, who is the wife of Mrida (Shiva).

५६५. मित्ररूपिणी

She who is the friend of everyone (universe).

**नित्यतृप्ता भक्तनिधिर् नियन्त्री निखिलेश्वरी ।**\
**मैत्र्यादि-वासनालभ्या महाप्रलय-साक्षिणी ॥११५॥**

५६६. नित्यतृप्ता

She who is eternally contented (satisfied).

५६७. भक्तनिधिर्

She who is the treasure of the devotees.

५६८. नियन्त्री

She who controls and guides all beings on the right path.

५६९. निखिलेश्वरी

She who is the goddess of everything.

५७०. मैत्र्यादिवासनालभ्या

She who is to be attained by love and other good dispositions like friendship.

५७१. महाप्रलयसाक्षिणी

She who is witness to the great dissolution.

**पराशक्तिः परानिष्ठा प्रज्ञानघन-रूपिणी ।**\
**माध्वीपानालसा मत्ता मातृका-वर्ण-रूपिणी ॥११६॥**

५७२. पराशक्तिः

She who is the original and the final strength, the supreme power.

५७३. परानिष्ठा

She who is the personification of dedication.

५७४. प्रज्ञानघनरूपिणी

She who is the personification of all condensed wisdom.

५७५. माध्वीपानालसा 

She who is not eager for anything else, due to drinking of wine.

५७६. मत्ता 

She who is intoxicated.

५७७. मातृकावर्णरूपिणी

She who is in the form of the letters of the alphabet, she who is in the form of all colours and shapes.

**महाकैलास-निलया मृणाल-मृदु-दोर्लता ।**\
**महनीया दयामूर्तिर् महासाम्राज्य-शालिनी ॥११७॥**

५७८. महाकैलासनिलया

She who resides in the great mountain of Kailash.

५७९. मृणालमृदुदोर्लता

She whose arms are as soft and cool as the lotus stem.

५८०. महनीया 

She who is adorable.

५८१. दयामूर्तिर्

She who is the personification of compassion.

५८२. महासाम्राज्यशालिनी

She who is the empress of all worlds.

**आत्मविद्या महाविद्या श्रीविद्या कामसेविता ।**\
**श्री-षोडशाक्षरी-विद्या त्रिकूटा कामकोटिका ॥११८॥**

५८३. आत्मविद्या

She who is the knowledge of the self.

५८४. महाविद्या

She who is the seat of all exalted knowledge.

५८५. श्रीविद्या

She who is the goddess of sacred knowledge.

५८६. कामसेविता 

She who is worshipped by Kama, the god of love.

५८७. श्रीषोडशाक्षरीविद्या

She who is in the form of the sixteen-syllabled mantra.

५८८. त्रिकूटा

She who is in the three parts (of the pa~nchadashi mantra).

५८९. कामकोटिका

She, of whom Kama (Shiva) is a part or an approximate form; she who sits on the Kama Koti peetha.

**कटाक्ष-किङ्करी-भूत-कमला-कोटि-सेविता ।**\
**शिरःस्थिता चन्द्रनिभा भालस्था इन्द्रधनुःप्रभा ॥११९॥**

५९०. कटाक्षकिङ्करीभूतकमलाकोटिसेविता

She who is attended by innumerable Laksmis who yearn for her mere glances.

५९१. शिरःस्थिता

She who resides in the head.

५९२. चन्द्रनिभा

She who is resplendent like the full moon.

५९३. भालस्था 

She who resides in the forehead (between the eyebrows).

५९४. इन्द्रधनुःप्रभा 

She who is resplendent like the rainbow.

**हृदयस्था रविप्रख्या त्रिकोणान्तर-दीपिका ।**\
**दाक्षायणी दैत्यहन्त्री दक्षयज्ञ-विनाशिनी ॥१२०॥**

५९५. हृदयस्था 

She who resides in the heart.

५९६. रविप्रख्या

५९७. त्रिकोणान्तरदीपिका

५९८. दाक्षायणी

५९९. दैत्यहन्त्री 

She who is the killer of asuras.

६००. दक्षयज्ञविनाशिनी

She who is the destroyer of the sacrifice conducted by Daksha.

**दरान्दोलित-दीर्घाक्षी दर-हासोज्ज्वलन्-मुखी ।**\
**गुरुमूर्तिर् गुणनिधिर् गोमाता गुहजन्मभूः ॥१२१॥**

६०१. दरान्दोलितदीर्घाक्षी

६०४. गुणनिधिर्

६०५. गोमाता

६०६. गुहजन्मभूः 

She who is the mother of Guha (Subramanya).

**प्रतिपन्मुख्य-राकान्त-तिथि-मण्डल-पूजिता ॥१२२॥**

६०९. दहराकाशरूपिणी

She who is worshipped daily on all fifteen days from full moon to new moon. 

**कलात्मिका कलानाथा काव्यालाप-विनोदिनी ।**\
**सचामर-रमा-वाणी-सव्य-दक्षिण-सेविता ॥१२३॥**

६११. कलात्मिका

She who is the atma of all the kala (arts).

६१२. कलानाथा

६१३. काव्यालापविनोदिनी

६१४. सचामररमावाणीसव्यदक्षिणसेविता

६१५. आदिशक्तिर्

८२०. सती

She who transcends the gunas.

९६२. सर्वातीता

She who transcends everything.

९६३. शमात्मिका

She who is the embodiment of peace and bliss.

बन्धूक-कुसुमप्रख्या बाला लीलाविनोदिनी ।\
सुमङ्गली सुखकरी सुवेषाढ्या सुवासिनी ॥१७७॥

९६४. बन्धूककुसुमप्रख्या 

She who resembles the Bandhooka flower in beauty, glitter and grace.

९६५. बाला 

She who never forsakes the nature of a child.

९६६. लीलाविनोदिनी

She who delights in her divine play.

९६७. सुमङ्गली 

She who is eternally auspicious; she who is the giver of good things. 

९६८. सुखकरी 

She who gives pleasure.

९६९. सुवेषाढ्या 

She who is very attractive.

९७०. सुवासिनी

She who is ever auspiciously married, she whose fragrance is sweet.

सुवासिन्यर्चन-प्रीता ऽऽशोभना शुद्धमानसा ।\
बिन्दु-तर्पण-सन्तुष्टा पूर्वजा त्रिपुराम्बिका ॥१७८॥

९७१. सुवासिन्यर्चनप्रीता

She who is pleased by the worship performed by married women.

९७२. आशोभना 

She who is always radiant.

९७३. शुद्धमानसा 

She who is of pure mind; one who purifies the mind of her devotees.

९७४. बिन्दुतर्पणसन्तुष्टा

She who is pleased by offerings to the bindu (the dot in the  Shri Chakra).

९७५. पूर्वजा

She who precedes everyone; first born.

९७६. त्रिपुराम्बिका

She who is the mother of the tripuras (three cities).

दशमुद्रा-समाराध्या त्रिपुराश्री-वशङ्करी ।\
ज्ञानमुद्रा ज्ञानगम्या ज्ञानज्ञेय-स्वरूपिणी ॥१७९॥

९७७. दशमुद्रासमाराध्या 

She who is worshipped by ten mudras (sarva sankhobhinisa ,sarva vidravini, sarva karshini, sarvava shankari, sarvonmadini, sarva mahankusha, sarva khechari, sarva bija, sarva yoni, sarva trikhanda).

९७८. त्रिपुराश्रीवशङ्करी

She for whom Tripura Sree is under control.

९७९. ज्ञानमुद्रा 

She who is in the form of the j~nAna mudra.

९८०. ज्ञानगम्या 

She who is to be attained through the yoga of knowledge.

९८१. ज्ञानज्ञेयस्वरूपिणी

She who is both knowledge and the known.

योनिमुद्रा त्रिखण्डेशी त्रिगुणाम्बा त्रिकोणगा ।\
अनघा ऽद्भुत-चारित्रा वाञ्छितार्थ-प्रदायिनी ॥१८०॥

९८२. योनिमुद्रा

She who is in the form of the yonimudra.

९८३. त्रिखण्डेशी

She who is the goddess of the tenth mudra, the trikhanda; she who is the goddess of the three zones of fire, sun, and moon.

९८४. त्रिगुणाम्बा

She who is endowed with the three gunas of sattva, rajas and tamas.

९८५. अम्बा

She who is the mother of all.

९८६. त्रिकोणगा 

She who resides in the triangle.अमबा

९८७. अनघा 

She who is sinless.

९८८. अद्भुतचारित्रा

She whose deeds are marvelous.

९८९. वाञ्छितार्थप्रदायिनी

She who gives all what is desired.

अभ्यासातिशय-ज्ञाता षडध्वातीत-रूपिणी ।\
अव्याज-करुणा-मूर्तिर् अज्ञान-ध्वान्त-दीपिका ॥१८१॥

९९०. अभ्यासातिशयज्ञाता

She who is attained through the exceedingly streneous practice of spiritual discipline.

९९१. षडध्वातीतरूपिणी

She whose form transcends the six paths.

९९२. अव्याजकरुणामूर्तिर्

She who is pure compassion, she who shows mercy without reason.

९९३. अज्ञानध्वान्तदीपिका

She who is the bright lamp that drives away ignorance.

आबाल-गोप-विदिता सर्वानुल्लङ्घ्य-शासना ।\
श्रीचक्रराज-निलया श्रीमत्-त्रिपुरसुन्दरी ॥१८२॥

९९४. आबालगोपविदिता

She who is worshipped by all, even by children and cowherds.

९९५. सर्वानुल्लङ्घ्यशासना

She whose commands can never be disobeyed by anyone.

९९६. श्रीचक्रराजनिलया

She who abides in Shri Chakra, the king of all chakras.

९९७. श्रीमत्त्रिपुरसुन्दरी

She who is the divine beautiful of wealth who is the wife of the God of Tripura (Shiva).

श्रीशिवा शिव-शक्त्यैक्य-रूपिणी ललिताम्बिका ।

९९८. श्रीशिवा

She who is the auspicious and divine Shiva; she who is the eternal bliss.

९९९. शिवशक्त्यैक्यरूपिणी

She who is the unification of shiva and shakti into one form.

१०००. ललिताम्बिका

She who is the divine mother Lalita who is easily approachable.
